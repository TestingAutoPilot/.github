# Support

- **A question about writing plans, or about a platform runner?** Open a **Discussion**
  on the relevant repo.
- **Found a bug — a step that fails, or renders or skips wrong on a backend?** Open an
  **Issue** on that repo, with a plan that reproduces it and the platform and version.
- **A security problem?** See **[SECURITY.md](SECURITY.md)** — report it privately, not
  as an issue.
- **What is this?** See the **[README](profile/README.md)**.
